At Saz’ isiXhosa, no Sapho, we invite learners of all ages to explore the beauty of the Xhosa language and culture. Choose your level—beginner, intermediate, or expert/native speaker—and answer a few questions to gauge your skills. You can also reveal the answers to start learning immediately.

Our site offers a warm, engaging space to learn through vocabulary, phrases, numbers, grammar, and cultural insights. From the joy of Ukuxoxa Iintsomi (telling folk tales) to practical language skills, we blend heritage with modern learning. Explore our Culture section for tales of Transkei or try our Activities to make learning fun. Let’s keep Xhosa thriving together!
