Welcome to Saz’ isiXhosa, no Sapho

Join Sazi and his cousins to learn Xhosa, the language of the Eastern Cape! Explore our lessons, activities, and cultural insights designed for learners of all ages.

Join Sazi and his cousins on a vibrant journey to learn Xhosa, the heart language of the Eastern Cape! At Saz’ isiXhosa, no Sapho, we invite learners of all ages to dive into our interactive lessons, engaging activities, and rich cultural insights. Whether you're a child discovering the rhythm of Xhosa words, a parent sharing traditions with your family, or an adult reconnecting with your roots, our site is a warm, welcoming space to explore.

Discover the beauty of Xhosa through vocabulary, phrases, numbers, and grammar, all woven with stories and traditions from our multigenerational family. From the joy of Ukuxoxa Iintsomi (telling folk tales) to practical language skills, we blend heritage with modern learning. Explore our Culture section for tales of Transkei or try our Activities to make learning fun. Let’s keep Xhosa thriving together!
